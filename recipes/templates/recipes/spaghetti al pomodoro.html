{% extends 'recipes.html' %} {% block title %}Spaghetti al Pomodoro (4 people){% endblock %} {% block ingredients %}
  • 500 g of spaghetti
  • 2 cans of cut tomatoes without skin (around 800g)
  • Olive oil
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • Few basil leaves
  • Optional parmigiano reggiano cheese (approx. 100g)
  • Optional teaspoon of sugar
    • {% endblock %} {% block directions %}
  • Coat the bottom of your sauce pan with olive oil and put on high heat.
  • Chop your garlic finely and put it in your sauce pan. Cook it without colour.
  • Start cooking you pasta and put your canned tomatoes into the pan with garlic.
    A good trick for hitting perfect donnes is taking your pasta out 1 minute earlier than it's said on the package and draining it.
    Later on we will put it into our sauce which will finish the cooking of the pasta.
  • Let the sauce cook for roughly 10 minutes on medium heat and let the water evaporate.
    It intensifies the flavour and make the sauce bit thicker.
  • Add a teaspoon of sugar. Salt and pepper is to your preference. Personally I add a pinch of both.
  • Drain your pasta and put it inside the pan with the sauce. Mix them well.
  • Your spaghetti is ready to be served. Put it on the plate, cut some basil and cheese, then sprinkle on top of it.
    • {% endblock %} {% block ending %}Enjoy your Spaghetti al Pomodoro{% endblock %}
