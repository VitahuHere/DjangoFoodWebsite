{% extends 'recipes.html' %} {% block title %} Pancakes {% endblock %} {% block ingredients %}
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour, (spooned and leveled)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, or vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Various toppings, such as butter, maple syrup, honey, jams, preserves, or chocolate syrup
    • {% endblock %} {% block directions %}
  • Preheat the oven to 200 degrees Celsius. Make sure to have something to put your ready pancakes.
  • Add and mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt to a small bowl. Leave it aside
  • Whisk milk, butter (or oil) and egg together in a medium bowl. Add contents of the previous bowl and mix them.
    Don't over do it. A few lumps are fine.
  • Moisten a baking tray with oil and carefully heat it up.
  • For each pancake put 2 to 3 tablespoons of batter. Spead it a little with the back of a spoon.
  • Bake for around 2 minutes or until you see some bubbles on top. Then flip the pancake and bake for another 2 minutes until brown.
  • Take out your ready pancakes and cover them to keep the heat. Serve with desired toppings.
    • {% endblock %} {% block ending %} Enjoy your pancakes {% endblock %}
