{% extends 'recipes.html' %} {% block title %}Polish boneless pork chop (schabowy){% endblock %} {% block ingredients %}
  • 600g boneless porkchop
  • Salt and pepper
  • Milk
  • All purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • Bread crumbs
  • For frying, butter or oil
    • {% endblock %} {% block directions %}
  • Trim the pork chop and cut to 4 slices.
  • Beat every slice until thin. It's best to use special hammer like tool.
  • Soak every slice in milk seasoned with salt and pepper. Leave it for 2 hours.
  • After soaking for 2 hours, take them out and dry them with a paper towel. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
  • Prepare 3 bowls for flour, whisked eggs and bread crumbs - one for each.
  • Take each slice and coat evenly with flour, then eggs, then bread crumbs.
  • Put butter (or oil) into a sauce pan and heat up on high power.
  • Place 2 slices into the pan and fry for around 2 minutes on high heat for each side, then another 6 minutes on medium heat for each side as well.
  • Place ready pieces onto a plate with paper towel to drain from excess oil.
  • Serve with mashed potatoes and misery (polish cucumber salad)
    • {% endblock %} {% block ending %} Enjoy your delicious lunch {% endblock %}
